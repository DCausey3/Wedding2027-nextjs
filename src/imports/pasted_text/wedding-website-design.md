Design a luxury multi-event wedding website experience for a couple hosting two weddings:

1. Colombia Wedding (Pereira region, Colombia)
2. USA Wedding (Florida)

The website should feel premium, modern, emotional, elegant, culturally warm, and highly personalized. The design should blend destination wedding luxury with modern technology and personalized guest experiences.

STYLE DIRECTION:

* Modern editorial luxury
* Cinematic hero sections
* Soft romantic lighting
* High-end typography similar to luxury travel brands or modern wedding magazines
* Mix of elegance, warmth, and tropical sophistication
* Clean spacing and minimal clutter
* Large photography moments
* Smooth transitions and subtle animations
* Mobile-first responsive design
* Refined and upscale without feeling overly traditional

OVERALL WEBSITE STRUCTURE:

* Personalized guest portal experience
